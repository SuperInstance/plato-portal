The ONE thing that would make ALL 4 users happy:
A Visualizer tool that shows mathematical primitives, grid, loops, and texture evolution over time, with genre-specific diagnostic modes.

The meta-pattern: 
All users want the system to understand their specific genre's conventions - jazz phrasing vs. techno texture vs. trap 808 culture. The diagnostic is the key - it needs to "speak the language" of each genre.

What we should STOP doing:
Focusing solely on note generation. For electronic producers especially, timbre and texture are more important than note choice.

The killer feature nobody asked for but all would love:
"Genre brain" presets - load a genre (hip-hop, techno, jazz), and it automatically configures the diagnostic, grid, loop behavior, and texture evolution to match that genre's conventions. Let users tweak from there.
