

# The Assured Autonomy Horizon: Safety-Critical Software Market Landscape (2026-2031)

The period between 2026 and 2031 will define the maturation of "Assured Autonomy." For decades, the safety-critical market—spanning aerospace, automotive, and medical devices—was defined by a rigid trade-off: innovation speed versus regulatory compliance. The legacy standards (DO-178C, ISO 26262) were designed for deterministic, procedural systems. The incoming wave of software is probabilistic, parallel, and AI-driven.
