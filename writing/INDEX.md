## 🎯 Reverse-Actualized Mechanisms

> Concrete, buildable ideas discovered inside speculative fiction.

1. **Compute allocation as civil rights** (2080 Shadow Dual) — α dial access determined by credit score is the new redlining. Audit any tiered AI system.
2. **Tiered precision as discrimination vector** (2080 Shadow Dual) — constraint checker set to lower precision for poor shells.
3. **Consensus-enforced inequality** (2080 Shadow Dual) — low-α groupthink: caseworker and client both at α=0.1 agree everything is "standard."
4. **Deadband dancers** (2042 Dial Goes to Zero) — resistance via spike-to-full-model in sampling gaps.
5. **Conservation as surveillance** (2042 Dial Goes to Zero) — spectral entropy patterns detect dissent.
6. **Sub-lattice spectral migration** (Experiment B) — weights drift between lattice points while eigenvalues stay conserved. Unexplored mathematical question.
7. **Helvetica Principle** (Experiment D) — system detects its own knowledge boundaries without inferring external content.
8. **Cross-room resonance** (Experiment F) — independent rooms converging on same lattice configuration creates side channels.
