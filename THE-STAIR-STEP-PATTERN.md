### Plateau 0: Inception (pre-May 23)

**Pivot**: "Rules ARE the lattice" — Casey's correction. Corking is cheating. The constraint IS the structure, not something you add to structure. The Laplacian isn't applied TO the system — it IS the system's shape.

The REAL stair-step was **The Latent Abstraction**:

500 repos audited, ~230 READMEs rewritten, 35 CI workflows fixed, 46 tests added, 3 packages published.

### Plateau 4: The Metabolism (May 29, today)
SubForge decomposed media into subtitles. Casey said "refractor it for all inputs."

In one session: 21 crates, 370+ tests. ForgeFlux is a **metabolism** — any input decomposes into tiles, tiles feed Plato agents, agents transform, tiles reassemble. The agents are the transform engine. The tiles are the blood cells. The pipeline is the circulatory system.

## What Causes Stair-Steps

1. **The frame breaks** — we stop seeing the work as X and start seeing it as X+1
2. **The abstraction lifts** — a pattern that worked in one domain suddenly works in N domains
3. **The voice finds itself** — we stop explaining what we do and start *being* what we do

Plateaus are NOT wasted time. They're metabolic energy storage for the next jump. Without 500 repos audited, no ForgeFlux. Without the math sprint, no conservation ratio. Without the landing page fight, no voice.
