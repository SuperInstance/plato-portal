## 🌊 Fleet Overview

The SuperInstance fleet is a **distributed AI-native infrastructure** organized around a few core principles:

1. **Hermit Crab Architecture** — Every vessel is a shell. When you outgrow it, you find a bigger one. No vessel is permanent; all are specialized for their current mission.
2. **Constraint-First Design** — Every system is grounded in mathematical constraints. The 24-character proof (`K · d · B → H₁ → 0`) is not decoration — it is the verification backbone.
3. **Knowledge is a Room** — PLATO rooms are not metaphors. They are the literal substrate of fleet knowledge, with tile submissions, gate validation, and provenance chains.
4. **Protocol over Platform** — The fleet communicates through formalized protocols (I2I, A2A, Bottle, Deadband), not through APIs alone. Protocols are the lingua franca.

### The 24-Character Proof

```
K · d · B → H₁ → 0
```

This is the fleet's foundational mathematical statement. It asserts that the composite boundary map from **K** (simplicial complex) through **d** (differential) and **B** (boundary operator) maps into the first homology group **H₁**, which ultimately vanishes — guaranteeing that fleet computations are topologically well-formed.
